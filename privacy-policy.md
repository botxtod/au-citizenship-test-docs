# AU Citizenship Test 2026 — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-04

This app is published by quafoo. We respect your privacy and have designed AU Citizenship Test 2026 to collect as little personal data as possible.

## What we collect

**Anonymous usage events** — When the "Allow analytics" setting is on (default), the app sends anonymous events to PostHog: which mode you opened, whether a question was answered correctly (with the question id and chapter id), mock-exam scores, and paywall views. These events do not include your name, email, Apple ID, IDFA, or any other identifier that could be used to identify you. PostHog assigns an opaque distinct ID to your install; that ID is not linked to any identity.

**Crash reports** — When the app crashes or hits an unrecoverable JavaScript error, the technical details (stack trace, device model, OS version, app version) are sent to Sentry so we can fix the bug. Crash reports do not include your answers, scores, or settings.

**In-app purchase receipts** — When you purchase Premium, your Apple ID receipt is processed by Apple and validated by RevenueCat (our IAP provider). We do not store your Apple ID or receipt content; RevenueCat receives only the receipt and the entitlement state.

## What we do NOT collect

- No name, email, phone number, address, or other contact details
- No IDFA, advertising ID, or other cross-app tracking identifier
- No location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access
- No transcripts of your in-app feedback emails (the `mailto:` flow opens your own Mail app — we receive the email you choose to send, not anything captured automatically)

## How to opt out

Open **Settings → Allow analytics** and toggle it off. PostHog event sending stops immediately and does not resume until you toggle the setting back on. No queued events are sent later.

You can also delete the app to remove all on-device data. The app stores your progress, mistakes, stars, and theme preference locally in SQLite — no cloud sync.

## In-app purchases

Premium is a one-time $9.99 purchase that unlocks all chapters and unlimited mock exams. Apple processes the payment; RevenueCat manages the entitlement. The app reads your entitlement state at launch via the RevenueCat SDK; no other purchase data leaves your device.

You can restore a prior purchase via **Settings → Restore purchases**. This re-checks your Apple ID for the entitlement; nothing is stored or transmitted beyond what Apple/RevenueCat already does for the standard StoreKit flow.

## Children's privacy

This app is intended for adults preparing for the Australian Citizenship Test. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. The current policy is always available in the app at **Settings → Privacy policy**.
